Matthew McConaughey's Tuna Fish Salad Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Wasabi paste
  • Italian dressing
  • Lemon (for fresh juice)
  • White vinegar
  • Raw agave syrup
  • Canned tuna fish (preferably packed in water)
  • Dill pickles (finely chop them yourself)
  • Fried shallots (or pre-made crispy fried shallots)
  • Green apple (for chopping)
  • Canned corn (or frozen if you prefer)
  • Frozen peas
  • Sourdough bread (preferably thick slices for toasting)
  • Butter (for toasting the bread)
